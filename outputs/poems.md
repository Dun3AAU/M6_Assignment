## Danish Poem

I morgen blæser det stærkest i Hanstholm,
perfekt for kitesurf, med vind i håret.
Aalborg Øst og Skive er også fine steder,
men Hanstholm er bedst, med sine kraftige vinder.

## English Poem

Tomorrow's winds will blow strongest in Hanstholm's shore,
ideal for kitesurfing, with the breeze in your hair once more.
Aalborg Øst and Skive are also nice spots to be,
but Hanstholm's the best, with its powerful gusts set free.
